Welcome to TrafficSim.

TrafficSim is a web platform for research-oriented traffic simulation. Its purpose is to establish a platform for transportation researchers to collaborate and share their works. It allows usurers to build traffic simulation scenario, construct traffic simulation model, and run traffic simulation over the web.

A demo version has been completed and ready for testing, at http://54.69.148.32:8080/simframework/

If you're interested collaborating or access to source codes, please contact author Xuan Shi at bennieys@gmail.com for details.
